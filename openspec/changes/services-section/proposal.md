## Why

The #uslugi section is currently an empty placeholder. Visitors who scroll past the hero need to immediately understand what services are offered. A clear, visual services grid builds trust and helps visitors self-identify as the right customer.

## What Changes

- Replace the empty `#uslugi` placeholder in `index.astro` with a `Services.astro` component
- Build a responsive card grid with one card per service (icon + title + description)
- All 7 services from the PRD represented
